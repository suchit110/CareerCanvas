Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using CareerCanvas and job searching

About CareerCanvas

CareerCanvas is a comprehensive job portal that connects job seekers with employers. Our platform offers tools for job searching, application tracking, career resources, and more to help you advance in your career journey.
Yes, CareerCanvas offers a free basic account for job seekers. Premium features may be available for a subscription fee. Companies and recruiters may have different pricing plans for posting jobs and accessing candidate databases.
CareerCanvas is designed for both job seekers and employers. Whether you're a recent graduate, experienced professional, career changer, or a company looking to hire talent, our platform has features to meet your needs.

Job Search & Applications

You can search for jobs using the search bar on our homepage. Enter keywords, job titles, or company names, and you can filter results by location, job type, experience level, and more. You can also browse jobs by category from our job categories section.
Once you find a job you're interested in, click on the "Apply Now" button on the job listing. You'll need to have an account and be logged in. Some applications may be completed directly on CareerCanvas, while others may redirect you to the employer's website.
Yes, you can track all your applications through your profile dashboard. You'll be able to see the status of each application, any messages from employers, and set reminders for follow-ups.

Account & Profile

Click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation bar. You can create an account using your email address and a password. Fill in the required information and follow the prompts to complete your registration.
Log in to your account and navigate to your profile page. Click on the "Edit Profile" button to update your personal information, work experience, education, skills, and other details. Don't forget to save your changes.
To delete your account, go to your profile settings and look for the "Account Management" section. There should be an option to delete your account. Please note that this action is permanent and all your data will be removed from our system.

For Employers

Log in to your employer account and click on "Post a New Job" from your dashboard. Fill in the job details including title, description, requirements, benefits, and application instructions. Review and publish your listing when ready.
You can view and manage all applications through your employer dashboard. Sort and filter candidates, add notes, change application statuses, and communicate with applicants directly through our platform.
We offer various subscription plans for employers based on your hiring needs. Plans range from basic job postings to premium packages with features like featured listings, candidate search access, and analytics. Contact our sales team for detailed pricing information.
