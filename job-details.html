Senior UX Designer

Hooli

San Francisco, CA Full Time Senior Level $120,000 - $150,000 Posted 3 days ago
Apply Now

Job Description

Hooli is looking for a Senior UX Designer to join our product team. As a Senior UX Designer, you will play a crucial role in shaping the user experience of our flagship product. You'll collaborate with product managers, engineers, and other designers to create intuitive and engaging user interfaces.

This position requires a deep understanding of user-centered design principles, strong visual design skills, and the ability to translate complex requirements into simple, elegant solutions. The ideal candidate will have experience designing for web and mobile applications, with a portfolio that demonstrates a thoughtful approach to problem-solving.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the design process from concept to implementation, including user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design
  • Collaborate with product managers to define product requirements and develop user-centered design solutions
  • Work closely with engineering teams to ensure design implementation meets quality standards
  • Conduct user testing and incorporate feedback into design iterations
  • Create and maintain design systems and pattern libraries
  • Mentor junior designers and provide constructive feedback
  • Stay current with UX trends, tools, and best practices
  • Advocate for user needs throughout the product development cycle

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in UX/UI design, preferably for web and mobile applications
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design process and problem-solving abilities
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or similar
  • Experience with prototyping tools (InVision, Principle, Framer, etc.)
  • Excellent visual design skills with a keen eye for detail
  • Strong communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams
  • Bachelor's degree in Design, HCI, or a related field (or equivalent experience)

Benefits

  • Competitive salary range ($120,000 - $150,000) based on experience
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) matching program
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options
  • Generous paid time off policy
  • Professional development budget
  • Employee stock option plan
  • Modern office in downtown San Francisco with snacks, meals, and recreational activities
  • Regular team outings and events
